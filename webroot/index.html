How to Play

Word Arena features a daily rotating game mode. A new mode appears each day at midnight UTC. Play every day to build your streak!
🏰 Word Conquest ▶
You'll see a 6x6 grid of tiles, each hiding a scrambled word
Start in one corner — tap a tile next to your territory to try it
Unscramble the word correctly and that tile becomes yours
Keep expanding to capture as many tiles as you can in 3 minutes
Tip: Push toward the center early so you have more tiles to reach
🔐 Cipher Breaker ▶
You'll be given 5 coded words to decode, one at a time
Each stage gets harder — scrambles, missing vowels, shifted letters, then cryptograms
Stuck? You have 3 hints to reveal letters
Score is based on stages cleared and how fast you finish
Tip: Save your hints for the last couple of stages
📈 Word Stock Market ▶
You get 9 random letters, each with a price that changes daily
Type words using those letters — longer words pay way more
You have 2 minutes to earn as much as you can
Your final score is your total earnings
Tip: Look for long words first — they're worth the most
⚔️ Lexicon Dungeon ▶
Explore a 10-floor dungeon — each floor has a monster blocking your path
Spell words to attack — longer words deal more damage
Every 3 floors you'll find a health potion to heal up
Floor 10 has a boss — you need 6+ letter words to hurt it
Tip: Don't waste short words early — save your best for the boss
